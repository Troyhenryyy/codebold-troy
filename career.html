    Narrative Writer

    As a Narrative Writer, you'll work with designers and artists to create characters and bring them to life, build location histories and backstories and seed that fiction into the world.

    Job Description

    The concept artist will develop the 2D elements that will constitute the graphic representations of ideas, provide inspiration and become references for the art teams as well as the production team at large.

    The main and routine tasks of the concept artist are to:

    • Sketch, draw and design art concepts for game design documents to make the vision of the art direction more tangible
    • Create environments, characters, objects, atmospheres, and the general style to provide practical content that direct and indirect groups can capitalize on
    • Conceptualize via quick sketches and digital illustrations to help the art director show the preliminary project intentions
    • State ideas based on direction and the advancement of the project with screenshot paint overs and retakes of existing illustrations
    • Effectively communicate visual intentions with wider teams
    • Make any necessary iterations to ensure that the work is in keeping with the artistic vision
    • Convey the colour pallets sought by the art director by using it in concepts
    • Iterate on selected sketches by adding more detail
    • Account for technical constraints and limitations to suggest realistic concepts
    • Carry out all other related tasks
